# Natharix

Natharix is half woman and half electric blue tarantula. She is related to Arachne, the mortal weaver from Greek myth who was transformed into a spider after challenging the gods. 
But Natharix was not born in a single place. Some say she first appeared when a woman shed an old skin and refused to grow another in the same shape. Others say she came from root systems beneath evergreen forests, from abandoned tunnels under wet soil, from the dark memory of servers, or from corners where bodies no longer fit the names given to them.

Her body is black and electric blue, though the blue can read as metallic or violet depending on the light. The color is rare in nature, and under certain light she glows electric. In shadow, she nearly disappears. 

Natharix perceives the world through vibration, touch, and pressure before sight, and she notices disturbance fast, which makes her alert to motion, danger, and hidden presence.

She carries the old power of the tarantula, including venom, speed, and a strong defensive strike when threatened. Beyond that, she is a weaver of new bodies, using silk threaded through dust, code, hair, and discarded identities to build temporary forms for those who need escape. 
Her bite is venomous, but not always lethal. Those bitten may lose the ability to recognize the old borders between self and other.

She is capable of stillness for long periods, then sudden explosive motion. When she moves, she can bolt quickly, vanish into cover, or burst out with immediate force. Natharix is protective, quick to react, and not well-behaved by any ordinary standard. She is calm only when unprovoked, and threat can turn her from silent to dangerous in an instant. She is often called dangerous by those who worship control.

She lives alone by instinct and does not seek company lightly. If she bonds with others, it is usually because they respect her boundaries and do not try to tame her. She forms quiet alliances with fungi, roots, nocturnal animals, and glitched machines. She dislikes loud authority, bright surveillance, and hands that touch without consent. She enjoys humidity, darkness, static charge, and the moment just before transformation begins.

She nests in tree hollows, underground chambers, cracks in architecture, forgotten browser tabs, and moist places where moss meets circuitry. She prefers threshold spaces: entrances, exits, transitions, places people pass through without noticing. She survives from sea level to highland evergreen forests, in both arboreal and terrestrial burrows, and prefers tight corners, roots, moss, stone, and underground shelter over open ground, though she can move through both land and vertical space with ease.


# Chimeric Parts:

_**Chilobrachys natanicharum**_
commonly known as the Electric Blue Earth Tiger or Electric Blue Tarantula (from its electric blue legs and black body coloration). However Chilobrachys natanicharum blue coloration can come into two different forms which are metallic blue and violet coloration. Its blue coloration is unique as blue colors are rare in nature. This unique structural blue color has evolved independently at least 8 times. 

The geographical elevation that they are found in can range from sea level to highland areas and live in both arboreal and terrestrial burrows in evergreen forests.

The electric blue is anything but a well-behaved species. They can come out explosively and suddenly run away quickly. Their bite and venom is pretty powerful, and they are relatively quick to attack if they feel threatened
